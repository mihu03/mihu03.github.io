    Welcome Message

    Dear colleagues,

    I'm thrilled to welcome you to the First International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Computing (AIC 2025), taking place from October 22th to 24th at Xinxiang, Henan, China.

    AIC 2025 serves as a high-level international forum where innovative academics and industrial experts come together to present their latest research advances in the fields of artificial intelligence and computing. Our conference brings together leading researchers and practitioners from around the world to explore groundbreaking developments across a wide spectrum of AI and computing disciplines.

    The conference features an exceptional program including keynote speeches by world-renowned scholars, regular sessions covering broad technical areas, and special sessions focusing on popular topics. Our comprehensive scope encompasses everything from artificial intelligence and machine learning theory to intelligent manufacturing, computer vision, and cryptographic applications.

    Xinxiang, with its blend of modern development and cultural heritage, provides an ideal setting for this prestigious gathering. Join us to share insights, forge collaborations, and shape the future of artificial intelligence and computing technologies.

    We look forward to welcoming you to AIC 2025 in Xinxiang, Henan, China, for what promises to be an enlightening and productive conference experience.

    Looking forward to meeting you in Xinxiang!

    Warm regards,

    Professor Shuxia Feng

    Professor Yiliu Tu

    Conference Committee General Chair, AIC2025
