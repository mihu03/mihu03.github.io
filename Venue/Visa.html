Visa Information for International Conference in Xinxiang

New Policy Announcement 144-hour Visa-Free Transit Available!

We are delighted to inform you that starting from July 15, 2024, international attendees can utilize China's 144-hour (6-day) visa-free transit policy to attend our conference in Xinxiang through Zhengzhou Xinzheng International Airport (CGO). This policy allows you to stay anywhere within Henan Province.

Enhanced Immigration Services at Zhengzhou Xinzheng International Airport. The airport has implemented several improvements to ensure smooth arrival:

  • Dedicated green channels for visa-free transit.
  • Clear signage and information boards.
  • Special 144-hour visa-free processing lanes.
  • Emergency service channels.
  • Optimized baggage check-through service for transit passengers.
  • Streamlined immigration procedures.

Eligible Countries

The 144-hour visa-free transit policy applies to passport holders from 54 countries:

European Countries (40)

Austria, Belgium, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, United Kingdom, Ireland, Cyprus, Bulgaria, Romania, Ukraine, Russia, Croatia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Montenegro, Serbia, Albania, North Macedonia, Monaco, Belarus, Norway<

American Countries (6)

United States, Canada, Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Chile

Oceanian Countries (2)

Australia, New Zealand

Asian Countries (6)

South Korea, Japan, Singapore, Brunei, United Arab Emirates, Qatar

Requirements

  1. Hold a passport from one of the eligible countries
  2. Have a valid international travel document
  3. Hold confirmed onward flight tickets to a third country/region (different from the country of origin)
  4. Stay within Henan province during the 144-hour period
  5. Enter through Zhengzhou Xinzheng International Airport (CGO)

Important Notes

The 144-hour period starts from 00:00 the day following your entry.

You must enter AND exit through Zhengzhou Xinzheng International Airport. You can freely travel within Henan province, including Xinxiang, during your stay.

Travel Information

  1. Airport Arrival

    • Look for the dedicated visa-free transit lanes at immigration
    • Present your onward flight ticket and conference invitation letter
    • Benefit from the new streamlined baggage check-through service

  2. Transportation to Xinxiang

    • Distance from Zhengzhou Airport to Xinxiang: approximately 65 km
    • High-speed train available from Zhengzhou to Xinxiang (30 minutes)

Alternative Option

If you plan to stay longer than 144 hours or prefer not to use the transit visa policy, you can apply for a regular visa at your local Chinese embassy or consulate. We will provide:

  • Conference invitation letter
  • Conference schedule

Contact Information

For visa and travel-related inquiries:

Conference Secretariat: aic2025.conf@gmail.com

Note: The new 144-hour visa-free transit policy makes it even more convenient to attend our conference. The organizing committee will assist you in arranging your schedule to maximize your participation while complying with immigration regulations.

Welcome Message

We warmly welcome international scholars to attend our conference in Xinxiang. The extended 144-hour visa-free transit period offers greater flexibility for your participation. Whether you choose to use the visa-free transit policy or obtain a regular visa, we look forward to providing you with a seamless conference experience in Henan Province.
