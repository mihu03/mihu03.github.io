Places To Visit

Wanxian Mountain Scenic Area (万仙山景区)

Step into one of the most spectacular mountain landscapes in Henan at Wanxian ("Ten Thousand Divinities") Mountain Scenic Area. This stunning destination is famous for its dramatic cliff faces and the remarkable "Guoliang Corridor" a 1,250-meter-long tunnel carved into the mountainside. The site offers breathtaking views of steep cliffs and unique rock formations, making it a popular spot for photography enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Guoliang Village (中华影视村-郭亮村)

Discover the fascinating Guoliang Village, known as China's "cliff village" and a popular filming location. This remarkable settlement is famous for its incredible location perched on the cliff edge and its historic hand-carved tunnel road. The village showcases traditional architecture and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains. The handmade road tunnel, built by local villagers, is considered one of China's most spectacular engineering feats.

Baligou Scenic Area (八里沟景区)

Explore the natural beauty of Baligou Scenic Area, a peaceful retreat known for its clear streams and pristine mountain landscapes. Located in a serene valley setting, this national tourist attraction features Buddhist architecture, tranquil waters, and lush forests. The area is particularly popular as a summer destination, offering visitors a cool escape with its natural environment and cultural sites.

Julian Mountain Scenic Area (九莲山景区)

Experience the majestic beauty of Julian Mountain Scenic Area, characterized by its impressive cliff faces, deep valleys, and cascading waterfalls. The area offers excellent hiking opportunities and spectacular viewpoints. With its diverse ecosystem and dramatic mountain scenery, it's an ideal destination for nature enthusiasts and photographers looking to capture the raw beauty of Henan's landscape.

Each of these destinations showcases the remarkable natural beauty and cultural heritage of the Xinxiang region, providing conference attendees with excellent opportunities to explore the area's stunning landscapes and rich history. These attractions are particularly notable for their unique geological features and cultural significance in the region.
